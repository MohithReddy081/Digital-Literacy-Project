# Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

1. **Verify URLs** before entering login credentials; avoid clicking suspicious links in emails or messages.  
2. **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on all important accounts, especially email and social media.  
3. **Use strong, unique passwords** and change them regularly.  
4. **Avoid sharing personal information** like phone number, address, or ID proofs on public platforms.  
5. **Monitor UPI and online banking transactions** daily and report any unauthorized transaction immediately.  
6. **Do not share OTPs or banking PINs** with anyone, even if they claim to be bank officials.  
7. **Keep your devices updated** with the latest security patches and antivirus software.  
8. **Report suspicious activity or cybercrime** to the **National Cyber Crime Portal** ([cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)) or call **Helpline 1930**.
